## Privacy Policy for ZenGrowth
#### Effective Date: March 14, 2026

### Introduction
ZenGrowth ("the Extension") is a productivity and focus-management tool developed to help users mitigate digital distractions. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your information.

By installing and using ZenGrowth, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

### Data Collection and Processing
ZenGrowth operates entirely on a local-first architecture. We do not collect, transmit, distribute, or sell any personally identifiable information (PII), browsing history, or analytics to external servers. All data generated by your use of the Extension is processed and stored locally on your personal device.

### Required Browser Permissions and Justifications
To provide its core functionality, ZenGrowth requests the following browser permissions. These permissions are strictly utilized within the local environment of your browser:

#### tabs:
Required to monitor the total number of open tabs (for the "Tab Pressure" feature) and to temporarily read the active tab's URL to compare it against your locally defined list of distracting websites. We do not track, log, or transmit your browsing history.

#### storage: 
Required to utilize chrome.storage.local. This allows the Extension to save your user-defined goals, custom image uploads, focus scores, and specific settings across browser sessions. This data never leaves your device.

#### sidePanel: 
Required to render the persistent visual interface (the digital bonsai tree and settings dashboard) alongside your active browsing window.

#### alarms: 
Required to execute background scripts at regular one-minute intervals to calculate and update your focus score without requiring continuous, resource-heavy processing.

### Third-Party Services
ZenGrowth does not integrate with any third-party analytics, tracking scripts, or advertising networks.

### Data Retention and Deletion
Because all user preferences, text inputs, and uploaded images are stored locally utilizing browser-level storage, you retain complete control over your data. You may delete all data associated with ZenGrowth at any time by uninstalling the Extension or clearing your browser's local extension data.

### Changes to this Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in the Extension's features or regulatory requirements. Any updates will be reflected by a revised "Effective Date" at the top of this document.
